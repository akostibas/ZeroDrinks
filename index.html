Download Zero Drinks Coming Soon

Key Features

  • Configurable sliding window (7, 14, or 30 days)
  • Automatically adds "0 drinks" for missing days
  • Calculates accurate averages
  • Positive messages for zero-drink days
  • Reflective prompts after drinking
  • Daily notifications with insights

How It Works

Run this shortcut once daily (set up an automation!). If you had zero drinks yesterday, you'll get a positive message. If you did drink, it asks "How do you feel?" along with your rolling average.

The shortcut ensures Apple Health has complete data by adding zero-drink entries for days you didn't log anything, giving you accurate trends and averages.

Adding to Home Screen

Long press the shortcut in the Shortcuts app, select "Details", then "Add to Home Screen". You can customize the icon and name.

Adding to Apple Watch

In the Watch app on your iPhone, scroll down to Shortcuts and enable "Show on Apple Watch". You can also add shortcuts to watch face complications.

Setting Up Automation

For Zero Drinks, create a daily automation in the Shortcuts app that runs at a specific time (like 9 AM each morning).

üîí Privacy First

Your Data Stays On Your Device

These shortcuts interact only with Apple Health on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers, no accounts required, no tracking. Your health information is yours alone.

Why These Shortcuts?

Apple Health is a powerful tool for tracking health metrics, but it lacks built-in support for quick drink logging and intelligent insights. These shortcuts fill that gap while maintaining your privacy.

The Zero Drinks shortcut was created to solve a specific problem: Apple Health ignores days without data, which skews averages and trends. By automatically filling in zero-drink days, you get accurate insights into your actual consumption patterns.
